## Content Types & Formats

### Blog Posts

**SEO-Optimized Guides (2,000-3,000 words)**
- "Complete Guide to [Topic]"
- "How to [Action] in 2024"
- "X Best [Tools/Methods] for [Audience]"

**List Posts (1,500-2,000 words)**
- "10 Ways to [Action]"
- "5 [Things] Every [Audience] Should Know"
- "X [Tools/Tips] for [Goal]"

**Case Studies (1,000-1,500 words)**
- "How [Creator] Uses ClipTune to [Result]"
- "Before & After: [Transformation Story]"
- "X% Increase in [Metric] Using ClipTune"

**News & Updates (500-1,000 words)**
- Feature announcements
- Industry news
- Company updates

---

### Video Content

**Tutorials (5-10 minutes)**
- Platform: YouTube
- Topics: How to use features, workflows, tips
- Format: Screen recording + voiceover

**Quick Tips (30-60 seconds)**
- Platform: TikTok, Instagram Reels
- Topics: Quick hacks, shortcuts, features
- Format: Fast-paced, engaging

**Behind-the-Scenes (2-5 minutes)**
- Platform: YouTube, Instagram
- Topics: Team, office, process
- Format: Casual, authentic

**User Testimonials (1-3 minutes)**
- Platform: YouTube, Website
- Topics: Success stories, use cases
- Format: Interview style
